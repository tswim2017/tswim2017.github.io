Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I register and pay onsite (at the conference)?

A: Yes. The conference only accepts cash for onsite registrations. However, we strongly encourage all participants to register and pay in advance. Early registration fees are offered at discounted rates.

What is the last day for conference registration via the website?

A: June 22, 2017.

Q: I have special dietary needs. How can I request special meals?

A: We are happy to honor your special dietary needs. Please indicate your request on your registration form prior to the conference. All special dietary requests must be made prior to the conference itself; requests made on site may not be honored.

Q: I have special dietary needs. How can I request special meals?

A: To amend your registration, please send an email to tswim2017.ncku@gmail.com, indicating the changes to be made.
