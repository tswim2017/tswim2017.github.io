Final Submission Guidelines

Deadline for camera-ready manuscripts: June 12, 2017

Submission via email

Please revise your accepted paper and email your camera-ready manuscript to tswim2017.ncku@gmail.com before June 12, 2017. Word file is required for submission. Late submission will not be accepted.

Formatting

  1. General Instructions:

    • Language: All manuscripts must be in English.

    • Paper Length: Papers are limited to 10 single-spaced pages in length, including title, the information of all author(s), abstract, all figures, tables, and references.

    • Page Setup: Your paper must be formatted for A4 size portrait page setup, with a 1-inch (2.54cm) margin (top, bottom, left and right).

    • Page Numbering: For the camera-ready manuscripts, please do not include page numbers.

  2. Make sure your paper is formatted as required by TSWIM 2017. Download the word template.

Papers that fail to follow the above instructions will be removed from the program and the conference proceedings without further notice.

Authorization form

A standard authorization form must be filled, signed and returned for each accepted paper. Only the principal co-author needs to sign. The TSWIM 2017 conference assumes that the signing author has the authorization from the remaining co-authors. You can download the authorization form here (MS WORD Version, PDF Version).

Please email your signed authorization form together with your camera-ready manuscript to tswim2017.ncku@gmail.com before June 12, 2017.

Registration

In order for your paper to appear in the conference program and proceedings, at least one author must register for the conference before June 12, 2017 and present the paper in the conference. Online registration is available now on the conference website at Registration page.

Enquires

If you have any questions about paper formatting, authorization form, and registration, please contact the workshop secretary at tswim2017.ncku@gmail.com.
